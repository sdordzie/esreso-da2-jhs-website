About Our School

ESRESO D/A 2 Junior High School is a public school located in Esreso, Bosomtwe District, Ashanti Region. We are committed to providing quality education under the Ghana Education Service (GES) and the Common Core Curriculum.

Our Teaching Staff

Subject Teachers

Total Staff

  • 2 support staff

Our Class Structure

We operate a triple-stream system from JHS 1 to JHS 3:

JHS 1

  • Class 1A (30 students)
  • Class 1B (30 students)
  • Class 1C (30 students)

JHS 2

  • Class 2A (30 students)
  • Class 2B (30 students)
  • Class 2C (30 students)

JHS 3

  • Class 3A (30 students)
  • Class 3B (30 students)
  • Class 3C (30 students)

Our History

Established in 2005, our school has served the Esreso community for nearly two decades, nurturing students to excel academically and morally.

Our Mission

"To empower students with knowledge, skills, and values for lifelong success through innovative teaching and community partnership."
