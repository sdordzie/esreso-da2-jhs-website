Welcome to Our School

This is the official website of ESRESO D/A 2 Junior High School.

Esreso D/A 2 JHS is a junior high government school located at Esreso. It is under Bosomtwe area of the Ghana Educational Service Bosomtwe District. It was established in 2005

MISSION

To ensure that all Ghanaian children of school growing age are provided with quality formal education and training Irrespective of their tribe, gender, disability, religion and a political affiliation.

VISSION

To provide relevant education to all learners at all levels to enable them to acquire skills that will assist them to develop their potentials in order to be productive in the society.

CORE VALUES

Discipline for Academic Excellence, Commitments, Honesty, Teamwork, Responsibility, Hard work and Self-respect.
