Academics & Curriculum

At ESRESO D/A 2 JHS, we follow the Common Core Programme (CCP) designed by NaCCA and approved by Ghana Education Service (GES) for Junior High Schools. Our curriculum is designed to develop well-rounded students with both academic knowledge and practical skills.

Subjects Offered

English Language

Developing reading, writing, speaking and listening skills

Mathematics

Numeracy, algebra, geometry and practical applications

Integrated Science

Biology, chemistry, physics and environmental science

Social Studies

History, geography, government and citizenship

Computing (ICT)

Digital literacy, computer skills and technology applications

Career Technology

Vocational skills and technical education

Creative Arts & Design

Visual arts, music, dance and drama

Religious & Moral Education

Ethics, religious studies and character development

Physical & Health Education

Sports, fitness and health education
