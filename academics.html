Academics & Curriculum

We follow the Common Core Programme (CCP) designed by NaCCA and implemented by Ghana Education Service (GES) for Junior High Schools. Our curriculum emphasizes both academic excellence and practical skills development.

Subjects Offered

English Language

Developing communication skills through reading, writing, speaking and listening

Mathematics

Building numerical literacy and problem-solving skills

Integrated Science

Exploring biological, physical and environmental sciences

Social Studies

Understanding Ghana's history, geography and civic responsibilities

Computing (ICT)

Developing digital literacy and technology skills

Career Technology

Practical vocational and technical education

Creative Arts & Design

Exploring visual arts, music, dance and drama

Religious & Moral Education

Developing ethical values and religious understanding

Physical & Health Education

Promoting fitness, sports and healthy living
