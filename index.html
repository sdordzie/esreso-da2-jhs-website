Welcome to Our School

Esreso D/A 2 JHS is a junior high government school located at Esreso. It is under Bosomtwe area of the Ghana Educational Service Bosomtwe District. Established in 2005, our school has been committed to providing quality education to the youth of our community.

Our Mission

To ensure that all Ghanaian children of school-going age are provided with quality formal education and training irrespective of their tribe, gender, disability, religion, or political affiliation.

Our Vision

To provide relevant education to all learners at all levels to enable them to acquire skills that will assist them to develop their potentials in order to be productive in society.

Core Values

The foundation of our educational philosophy:

Discipline Academic Excellence Commitment Honesty Teamwork Responsibility Hard Work Self-respect
